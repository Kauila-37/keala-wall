Waimea / Kamuela

North · Upcountry · Hawai'i Island
Reviewed July 2026 · Keala M. Wall

Cool ranch country with the island's best-rated schools and a paniolo heritage.

At a Glance

ClimateCool, variable, 50–75°F
Annual rainfall25–50 in (east side wetter)
Lava zoneZone 7–8 (low risk)
Median home price$650K–$900K
Elevation~2,500 ft
SchoolsBest rated on the island
Drive to Kona / Hilo~45 min each

The Honest Trade-Off

Waimea trades sunshine for climate and community: fog, sweaters, and green pasture instead of beach heat. It is central — about 45 minutes to either Kona or Hilo — which is a real advantage if your life pulls in both directions, and a commute either way if it doesn't.

Who It Suits

Families who prioritize schoolsRanchers and agricultural buyersBuyers who want cool weather
